About Me

Jeremiah Iromaka is a Software Engineer currently based in London, United Kingdom. With an illustrious career spanning over 6 years, Jeremiah has consistently demonstrated an unwavering commitment to elevating human experiences through cutting-edge technology and innovative solutions.

Jeremiah's journey has been characterized by a dynamic blend of building, guiding, and optimizing software development processes. Beyond his technical prowess, Jeremiah is a mentor, a volunteer, and an advocate for quality education. His contributions as a Full Stack Development and Data Structure Algorithm Tutor with Zuri Team, Inc. have been instrumental in shaping the next generation of developers. Through practical guides and publications on topics like corporate governance and firm innovation, Jeremiah shares his invaluable insights with the global community.

Jeremiah's educational background, including a Master of Science in Entrepreneurship and Innovation Management from Loughborough University London, provides a solid academic foundation for his practical work in the field. His experiences have equipped him with a diverse skill set and a global perspective. In addition to his technical prowess, Jeremiah is deeply passionate about innovation. His certifications in areas such as Executive Management Training and Enterprise Design Thinking Practitioner at IBM reflect his dedication to continuous learning and professional development.

Above all, Jeremiah's impact is felt not only in the projects he contributes to but also in the lives he touches through his mentorship and guidance. His vision for a more innovative and interconnected world serves as an inspiration.
